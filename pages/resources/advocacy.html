Resources · Advocacy

STEM Advocacy & the RSAA

We started advocating in the summer of 2022 to secure shop space — and it grew into a national alliance.

Team 2412 began focusing on advocacy in the summer of 2022 with the goal of securing shop space within our school district. Since then our goals have expanded: earning elective and CTE credit for robotics, funding STEM education, and ensuring everyone can pursue their love of STEM.

The RSAA

Out of that work we founded the Robotics STEM Advocacy Alliance (RSAA) — an organization that assists robotics teams in advocating for their needs by providing resources and a community to share and learn from one another.

Together with the RSAA, we present an annual RSAA Advocacy Conference teaching teams the tools for securing shop space, government funding, and developing connections through advocacy.

What we’re asking for

  • CTE and/or elective credit for active participation in robotics — the same way high-school sports earn PE credit that counts toward graduation
  • Dedicated, permanent shop space for robotics teams within school districts
  • Sustained government and district funding for STEM education
  • A community where teams share advocacy resources and learn from each other
