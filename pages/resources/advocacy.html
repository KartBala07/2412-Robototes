STEM Advocacy & the RSAA

since summer 2022

Team 2412 began advocating in the summer of 2022 with one goal: secure shop space within our school district. The mission has grown — earning elective and CTE credit for robotics, funding STEM education, and making sure everyone can pursue their love of STEM.

The RSAA

Out of that work we founded the Robotics STEM Advocacy Alliance — a community that helps robotics teams advocate for their needs with shared resources and experience. Together we host an annual RSAA Advocacy Conference teaching teams how to win shop space, government funding, and connections.

What we're asking for

  • CTE and/or elective credit for robotics participation — the way school sports earn PE credit toward graduation
  • Dedicated, permanent shop space for robotics teams in school districts
  • Sustained government and district funding for STEM education
  • A community where teams share advocacy playbooks and learn from each other
