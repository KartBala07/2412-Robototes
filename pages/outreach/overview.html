Outreach

STEM for everyone

One of our goals as a team is to connect with our community through STEM. At outreach events we demo our robots, bring hands-on engineering activities for kids, and talk about what we do as part of FIRST — but the work goes far beyond demos.

Team building

Ramen Robotics 9036

With mentors, tools, and knowledge, we helped start FRC Team 9036 and got the kids of Big Picture excited about FIRST.

Service

Community cleanups

Alongside Overdue Seattle we’ve collected over 150 pounds of garbage since 2022.

Education

Free weekly tutoring

A weekly free tutoring service supporting up to a dozen youth per session.

Events

CIE MathComp

We helped run the Chinese Institute of Engineers MathComp, attended by roughly 1,000 kids.

Volunteering

Runs in the Family

Team members volunteered at the TikTok “Runs in the Family” community event.

Demos

Robot demos

We bring robots to fairs, schools, and community events across the Bellevue area — want us at yours?
