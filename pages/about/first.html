About · FIRST

What is FIRST?

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology — the world’s leading youth robotics community.

FIRST is a global nonprofit that engages kids in exciting, mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills — and just as importantly, self-confidence, communication, and leadership.

The FIRST Robotics Competition

The Robototes compete through FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition), a high-school level program where teams compete and cooperate with each other. Every year we receive a new challenge or game; after the game is revealed at kickoff, teams have a little over six weeks to design, build, and improve their robot — a period known as Build Season.

FRC is often described as “the hardest fun you’ll ever have.” Under strict rules, limited time, and limited resources, teams of students build and program industrial-size robots to play a difficult field game against like-minded competitors, while also raising funds, designing a team brand, and honing teamwork skills.

Gracious Professionalism

FIRST is built around the ethos of Gracious Professionalism® — doing high-quality work while treating each other with respect and kindness. On the field we compete fiercely; off the field we share parts, tools, code, and advice with any team that needs them.
