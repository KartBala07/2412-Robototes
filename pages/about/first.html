What is FIRST?

For Inspiration & Recognition of Science and Technology

FIRST is a global nonprofit that engages kids in mentor-based programs building science, engineering, and technology skills — along with confidence, communication, and leadership.

The FIRST Robotics Competition

The Robototes compete in FRC, the high-school level program where teams compete and cooperate. Every January a brand-new game is revealed at kickoff; teams then get a little over six weeks to design, build, and program an industrial-size robot — the stretch known as Build Season.

It’s often called “the hardest fun you’ll ever have”: strict rules, limited time and resources, plus fundraising, branding, and strategy on top of the engineering.

Gracious Professionalism

FIRST runs on Gracious Professionalism® — compete fiercely on the field, and share parts, tools, code, and advice with any team that needs them off it.
