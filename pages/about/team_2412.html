About the Robototes

The Robototes, FRC Team 2412, was founded in 2007 at Sammamish High School with just 12 students and 2 mentors, competing for the first time in 2008. Our workspace changed frequently in the early years until 2015, when the team settled into Sammamish High School’s engineering and robotics rooms. Since then enrollment has tripled — the team now runs roughly 65 students and 20 mentors.

We compete in the Pacific Northwest district of the FIRST Robotics Competition. Our most successful seasons were 2014, 2019, and 2023, in which we won a combined 83 matches and earned many awards. Across 18 seasons the team has entered 47 official events, won 27 awards, and made 5 trips to the FIRST Championship.

When & where we meet

We meet in the Sammamish High School engineering room after school on Mondays, and four days a week during build season. Prospective members and mentors are always welcome to drop by.
