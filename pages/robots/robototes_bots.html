Robototes Bots

Eighteen seasons of machines, from our 2008 rookie build to Phoenix.

2026 · REBUILT

Phoenix

Our strongest season yet: 39–18–0 in official play, ranked #7 in the Pacific Northwest district with 246 points, captained Alliance 3 to win the Bonney Lake District event.

2025 · REEFSCAPE

Impact Award Season

Recognized with the FIRST Impact Award at the 2025 Sammamish District event and at the Pacific Northwest District Championship — FIRST’s highest honor for community impact.

2024 · CRESCENDO

A rebuilding year

19–32–1 in official play and 34–53–1 overall. Every tough season teaches the next one.

2023 · CHARGED UP

Crane

One of our best seasons ever: 30–25–0 in official play, 64–46–1 overall, ranked #12 in the district with 203 points.

~2020

Heron

Our 13th robot — who, according to team lore, prefers to be called Harry.

2014 & 2019

Banner years

Together with 2023, our most successful seasons — 83 matches won across the three and a shelf full of awards.

2008

Rookie season

Twelve students, two mentors, one robot — the season that started it all.

Career numbers

  • 18 seasons competed since 2008
  • 47 official events entered
  • 27 awards won
  • 5 trips to the FIRST Championship
